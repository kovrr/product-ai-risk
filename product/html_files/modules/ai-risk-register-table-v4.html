How Priority Scoring Works
Data Sources
Our AI-powered recommendations are based on comprehensive analysis of:
  • OECD.AI - 9,678 incidents from international news (2020-present)
  • AIAAIC - 2,084 AI incidents with impact data (2012-present)
  • AI Incident Database - 1,249 documented incidents
  • Damien Charlotin Database - 488 legal cases with monetary penalties
  • MIT AI Risk Repository - 1,600+ risk classifications from 65 frameworks
  • MITRE ATLAS - Adversarial threat patterns for ML systems
Qualitative Assessment
Pattern-matches your scenario description against our incident database to:
  • Identify frequency trends (e.g., "340% increase since 2020")
  • Determine regulatory risk level based on EU AI Act classifications
  • Assess industry prevalence and sector-specific patterns
Quantitative Impact Estimation
Statistical analysis of similar incidents provides:
  • Median and average financial impact ranges
  • Regulatory fine estimates (GDPR, sector-specific laws)
  • Recovery time and operational disruption metrics
Tactical Recommendations (MITRE ATLAS)
Analyzes attack patterns to suggest:
  • Additional tactics found in X% of similar incidents
  • Supply chain compromise considerations
  • Most relevant attack vectors for your scenario type
Asset Validation
Reviews completeness of AI asset documentation:
  • Vendor and version information
  • API dependencies and integrations
  • Supply chain mapping for comprehensive risk assessment
Impact Type Coverage Analysis
Compares your impact type selections to incident patterns:
  • Identifies missing harm categories based on historical data
  • Suggests additional impact types found in 75%+ of similar cases
  • Ensures comprehensive risk categorization
